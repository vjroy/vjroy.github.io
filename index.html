Research

LLM Research

I made a research paper with MIT graduate Roger Jin. We created a benchmark, RouteEval, to help test over 10,000 evaluations of running routes on 13 different LLMs. Our results showed that GPT-5 was the most accurate by a significant margin with 65% accuracy, followed by Grok-4 (55%), and Gemini-2.5-pro (52%). We submitted the research paper to ACL ARR 2025 October.

Machine Learning

Credit Card Fraud Detection

In the summer of my senior year, I went to a summer program at Columbia University where I studied data science and machine learning. We covered a variety of topics including modeling, artificial intelligence, and linear algebra. For my final project, I partnered up with a friend to create a model that detects credit card fraud. We used a voter classifier combining Logistic Regression and a Random Forest model to make a successful model. We also used data-balancing techniques like SMOTE to train the model for better performance.
